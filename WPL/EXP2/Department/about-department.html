Medical Designations (Glossary)

MD
Doctor of Medicine – A medical degree for physicians.
RN
Registered Nurse – A nurse who has graduated from a nursing program and met the requirements outlined by a country or state licensing body.
Entomologist
Not a medical doctor; a scientist who studies insects (often confused with etymologist).
Resident
A physician who has finished medical school and is receiving training in a specialized area.
